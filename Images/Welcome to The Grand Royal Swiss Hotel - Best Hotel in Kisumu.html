The Grand Royal Swiss Hotel.

The Best Hotel in Kisumu

The Grand Royal Swiss Hotel is located in a serene environment with a splendid view overlooking the Nandi Hills. Located along the outskirts of Kisumu City, the hotel boasts of a total of 217 spacious rooms under 6 categories, including a presidential suite offering a unique blend of comfort and relaxation. The hotel is ideal for both business and leisure travelers.
